1. Brand Overview
1.1 What is the Brand?
Brand Name: Spray Lite
Spray Lite is an FMCG / Consumer Health Food Industrial/Manufacturing brand.
Brand Category & Industry:
FMCG / Food & Beverage / Health & Wellness
Brand Story / Background (Rectified):
Spray Lite is a culinary innovation brand designed to revolutionize healthy cooking. By
utilizing advanced aerosol technology (American formulation), the brand bridges the gap
between flavor and fitness, offering a direct alternative to traditional oils, butter, and ghee.
Unlike traditional pouring oils, Spray Lite focuses on portion control and calorie reduction.
Through a precise mechanism combining oil, soy lecithin, water, and propellant, it ensures
food doesn't stick while reducing fat intake by over 98% per serving. It is designed for the
modern health-conscious kitchen, catering to air-frying, baking, grilling, and everyday
cooking.
